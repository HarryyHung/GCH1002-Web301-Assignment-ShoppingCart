Product description: Kokeshi are hand-made Japanese wooden dolls and are known as one of the oldest and most important traditional folk art works in Japan.
Kokeshi symbolizes peace and luck. The Japanese believe that children who play Kokeshi will be blessed with health and protected by the gods.
Kokeshi wooden dolls are made from treated wood to ensure no termites and hygiene.
